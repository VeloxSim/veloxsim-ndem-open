Examples of commercial uses requiring a license include:

- Integration into an internal engineering or simulation pipeline.
- Robotics and autonomous machine development.
- Machine-material interaction simulation.
- AI or machine-learning training and validation.
- Synthetic data generation.
- High-throughput or distributed simulation.
- Digital-twin applications.
- Commercial research and development.
- Internal simulation infrastructure.
- Integration into a commercial product or service.

A commercial license may be required even where VeloxSIm NDEM is used solely within an organization's internal systems and is not redistributed to third parties.

Evaluation use must not form part of a production, operational, engineering, machine-learning training, data-generation, or commercial research workflow.
